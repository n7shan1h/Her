Your Physics Gameplan ✦
🎯
PYQs before theory
See what NEET actually asks before reading the chapter. You'll realize 70% of questions are doable — fear comes from imagining hard questions, not reading real ones.
📐
One formula, understood deeply
Don't memorize 20 formulas in a sitting. Take F = ma. Know what each symbol means. Draw it. Derive it. Apply it in 2 MCQs. Then move on. Quality over quantity.
🧩
Formula wall / phone wallpaper
Write all formulas from one chapter on a page. Photograph it as your lock screen. You'll see it 40 times a day without trying. This one habit = 20+ marks.
🔁
3 MCQs minimum, every single day
Not 30. Just 3 Physics MCQs daily. Consistency beats intensity here. In 60 days that's 180 solved questions — more than one full exam worth of practice.
📺
Physics Wallah (Alakh Sir) on YouTube
Watch the concept video before reading NCERT. His visual + bilingual approach makes circuits and mechanics feel obvious. Free and genuinely brilliant.
⏭️
Skip → Flag → Return in exam
Never freeze on a Physics question. Skip it immediately. Your brain will process it subconsciously while you answer Biology. Return with fresh eyes.
💯
Target 100–120 out of 180, not full marks
You don't need perfect Physics to crack NEET. Target Electrostatics + Current Electricity + Modern Physics + Optics — these 4 topics alone can give you 80+ marks.
🧠
Dimensional analysis as a lifeline
If you forget a formula in exam, derive it using dimensional analysis. This technique alone has saved thousands of students from losing Physics marks they would've otherwise gotten.
